Nook

What is Nook?

Nook is my passion project. As a Columbia student, one of my favorite things to do in the city is to find new places to enjoy a cup of coffee and get work done. I noticed that a lot of places, may seem like they are great for work at first glance but when you get there, order a coffee you realize there is a myriad of problems; The place is too busy, too loud, cant even find seating. There are no outlets, or now wifi. All these factors i took into account when creating this project.

You can even make it your own by adding your own ratings and reviews to the places you visit! If you feel as passionate about coffee and finding the perfect study environment as I do, please feel free to contribute to the project by adding your own spots or rating the ones that are already here.
